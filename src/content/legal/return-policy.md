This Return & Refund Policy applies to purchases made through **Yoventa Limited** (trading as **Yoventa**) at **yoventaltd.com** (the “Site”), unless a product page or order states different terms.

## 1. Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items generally must:

- Be returned within **14 days** of delivery (or the period stated at checkout for your order)
- Be **unused**, in **original packaging**, with tags and accessories included
- Not be listed as **non-returnable** (e.g., digital goods, hygiene products where opened, clearance items marked “final sale”)

## 2. How to start a return

1. Contact us at **operations@yoventaltd.com** or **09096361340** with your order number and reason for return.
2. We will confirm whether your item is eligible and provide return instructions, including the return address if applicable.
3. **Do not** send items back without approval, as we may not be able to process unapproved returns.

## 3. Condition of returned items

Returned products are inspected. We may refuse a refund or apply a restocking fee if:

- The item is damaged (except where damage occurred in transit and is documented)
- Parts, manuals, or packaging are missing
- The item shows signs of use beyond what is necessary to inspect it

## 4. Refunds

- If your return is approved, we will process a **refund to the original payment method** where possible.
- Refunds typically appear within **5–10 business days** after we receive and inspect the item, depending on your bank or card issuer.
- **Shipping costs** are usually non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective/wrong item as confirmed by us.

## 5. Exchanges

Exchanges depend on stock availability. Where we cannot exchange, we may offer a refund or store credit as agreed in writing.

## 6. Defective or wrong items

If you receive a **defective** or **incorrect** item, contact us within **48 hours** of delivery with photos and your order number. We will work with you on replacement, repair, or refund as appropriate.

## 7. Cancellations

- **Before shipment:** Orders may be cancellable if you contact us before dispatch; we will confirm by email.
- **After shipment:** Standard return rules apply once you receive the package.
